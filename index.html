Sign Up & Get Started!

Trove is reimagining the way teachers find learning tools to use in the K-12 digital classroom.

We are committed to helping you build a better classroom by making the process of choosing tech a little easier.

We work with hundreds of developers, manufacturers, tinkerers, and social innovators to refine and prescreen a collection of the best tech solution for education from around the world.

You choose which tech to pilot test in the classroom, then provide us with valuable feedback in exchange for rewards and incentives!

How It All Works

STEP 1
Create A Profile

Sign up to join our happy tech community. Tell us a little bit more about you and we can custom fit a set of solutions to your needs.

STEP 2
Pick A Product

View carefully selected & researcher approved edtech products to pilot in the classroom. Our experts will help pick out what we think you will like.

STEP 3
Test Out The Product

Let us know what you think! Once you've tried your new tech out give us your honest feedback on your experiences in the classroom.

STEP 4
Reap Your Rewards

Build credit to fund materials to support your practice! Take home the treasure! Exchange for incentives.
